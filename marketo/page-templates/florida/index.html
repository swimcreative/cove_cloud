Land Lease
Communities

To make owning a home in traditionally higher cost areas and neigbourhoods a reality, you can take advantage of living in a land lease community. This allows you to purchase a home with a monthly payment that can be much lower than you would pay for for renting, or for a more expensive, stick built home. Instead, you pay a small mortgage payment for your home, plus the site rent, which covers water, sewer, garbage, basic cable and internet. The best part, you don't pay annual real estate taxes on the land.*
